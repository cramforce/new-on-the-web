What is new on the Web

with Malte

In April 2019 my team asked me: "Hey, Malte, how do you stay so on top of what is happening on the web?"

For their personal wellbeing I didn't want to answer "Hang out on Twitter for 14 hours every day!"

So, instead I created this page, updated monthly, with the most interesting web related Tweets I've seen.

I typically present these with lots of commentary in an hour-long meeting with my team, but I hope it can be helpful for people without that extra context.
